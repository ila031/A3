BUILD YOUR OWN LANDSCAPE
  • Press F to change the frame border color
  • Use Left/Right arrow keys to change the sky
  • Use Up arrow key to change the ground color
  • Press "S"/"M" to toggle the sun/moon
  • Press "R" to toggle mountains
  • Press "T" to spawn trees
  • Press "C" to spawn clouds
  • Press "X" for it to snow
